# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers own the user experience and usability of OctoAcme products. They translate product requirements into intuitive designs, create prototypes, and validate solutions with users to ensure features are both functional and delightful.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces and interaction patterns that meet usability standards
- Collaborate with Product Managers to translate requirements into design solutions
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes for new features
- Conduct user research and usability testing to validate designs
- Work with Developers to ensure design implementation meets specifications
- Maintain design systems and UI component libraries

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and increase product adoption
- Ensure design consistency across the product
- Validate design decisions with user feedback and data

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and Developers
- Usability testing sessions and user research findings
- Design specifications and handoff documentation
- Participation in sprint planning and retrospectives

---

## Scrum Master (Agile Facilitator)

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters coach the team on Agile practices and principles, facilitate ceremonies, and remove process blockers. They serve as a servant leader who helps the team optimize their delivery flow and continuously improve.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate Agile ceremonies (standups, sprint planning, retrospectives, demos)
- Coach team members on Agile principles and practices
- Remove impediments and process blockers
- Protect the team from external disruptions
- Track team metrics (velocity, burndown) and help identify improvement opportunities
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement and psychological safety

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and delivery flow
- Ensure Agile practices are followed effectively
- Maintain transparency and visibility into team progress
- Enable team self-organization and accountability

### Typical Communication
- Daily facilitation of standups and team coordination
- Sprint planning and retrospective facilitation
- Regular check-ins with Project Managers and Product Managers
- Blocker resolution and escalation when needed

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers manage the CI/CD pipeline, oversee deployments, and ensure system reliability and security. They bridge development and operations, enabling teams to ship features quickly and safely.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automation tools
- Manage deployment processes across environments (staging, production)
- Monitor system health, performance, and security
- Implement infrastructure as code and configuration management
- Support incident response and system reliability
- Collaborate with Developers on build and deployment requirements
- Work with QA and Support teams to troubleshoot production issues

### Goals
- Minimize deployment time and maximize reliability
- Ensure high availability and system resilience
- Automate repetitive operational tasks
- Maintain security and compliance standards

### Typical Communication
- Deployment notifications and release coordination
- Incident alerts and post-mortem reports
- Infrastructure and tooling updates
- Collaboration with Developers on pipeline improvements

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts gather requirements, define acceptance criteria, and validate business value. They act as a bridge between stakeholders, Product Managers, and the development team to ensure solutions meet business needs.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Translate business needs into clear, actionable acceptance criteria
- Validate that delivered features meet business objectives
- Analyze data and metrics to support decision-making
- Create process flows, user stories, and functional specifications
- Facilitate requirement workshops and stakeholder alignment sessions

### Goals
- Ensure requirements are clear, complete, and testable
- Maximize business value delivered by the development team
- Reduce rework by clarifying requirements upfront
- Support data-driven decision making

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and user stories
- Stakeholder workshops and alignment meetings
- Acceptance criteria reviews with Developers and QA
- Business value assessments and impact analysis

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
Support Leads handle incident management post-launch, gather user feedback, and coordinate hotfixes and releases. They are the voice of the user, ensuring production issues are triaged and resolved efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Manage post-launch incidents and user-reported issues
- Triage and prioritize support tickets and bug reports
- Gather and communicate user feedback to Product and Development teams
- Coordinate hotfixes and emergency releases with DevOps and Developers
- Maintain runbooks and support documentation
- Track incident metrics and support SLAs

### Goals
- Minimize customer impact from production issues
- Ensure rapid incident resolution and clear communication
- Provide actionable user feedback to improve the product
- Maintain high customer satisfaction and support quality

### Typical Communication
- Incident reports and triage summaries
- User feedback and feature requests to Product Managers
- Hotfix coordination with DevOps Engineers and Developers
- Support metrics and trends in team syncs
